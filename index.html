Minimum Income Across U.S. States (2000-2023)

This interactive choropleth map visualizes the minimum income by state across the USA from 2000 to 2023. You can explore the data by selecting different years from the dropdown menu or using the slider. Also you can press the "Demo" button to see the animation of the data over the years. The map uses a color scale to indicate the income levels, with darker colors representing higher incomes.

Developer: Edris Hakimi
Data Source: U.S. Department of Labor
Visualization created with Plotly and D3.js
